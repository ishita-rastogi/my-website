Edusaur

Food Safety

Food Safety investigates the restaurant food safety scores for restaurants in San Francisco. Through the use of multiple Data Cleaning methods and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), I have cleaned and analyzed multiple data sources, utilizing a metric system that looks at the distribution of restaurant scores over time.

Technologies Used:

  • Python
  • NumPy
  • Pandas
  • Matplotlib
  • Seaborn
